Cleaning and Analysis

This SQL project focuses on cleaning and analyzing the Superstore dataset to uncover meaningful business insights. I began by identifying and removing duplicate records, handling null values, correcting data types, and standardizing category names for consistency. After cleaning, I performed exploratory queries to analyze sales performance by region, product category, and customer segment, as well as to calculate profit margins and shipping efficiency.

Conclusion

The analysis highlights clear patterns in customer behavior, product profitability, and regional performance. While overall sales remain strong, several categories and regions show declining profit margins due to high discounting. The dashboard makes it easy to identify top-performing segments and isolate loss-making products. These insights support data-driven decisions in pricing, inventory planning, and targeted marketing to improve overall profitability.

A Tableau Dashboard was created to visualize these findings interactively.
